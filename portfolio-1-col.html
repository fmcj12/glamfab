Project One

Stunning Candy Buffet

Candy buffets are becoming popular at weddings, parties and baby showers. I have showcased a stunning wedding candy bars to inspire you. When planning a candy buffet, do not focus on the quantity of candy needed per guest. Instead, think about how you can make your candy buffet beautiful. To make your wedding candy bar look amazing, start with the table and containers. Candy buffet specialists recommend taking 5 to 10 types of candy and buying 15 or 20 pounds of each. The next step is choosing the candy for the candy bar. Usually, it is the weddings theme or seasons colors that define the candy buffet. For example, if you are having an all white wedding, you will want an all white candy bar. Choose cool containers: different shapes and different heights. If possible, choose candy containers that match the floral vases. Clear containers are the best.
